Thank you so much for buying Mic Drop! ❤️ Your purchase supports independent software developers and helps fund new features. 

## Getting your license

You should receive a receipt and a license key via email momentarily. If it doesn't come through (check your spam folders just in case!), please [drop us a line](mailto:help@getmicdrop.com) and we'll send it over manually. 

## How to activate your license

1. <DownloadLink>Download Mic Drop</DownloadLink> and install it, if you haven't already.
2. Open Mic Drop and click on the mic icon in the menu bar to open the menu.
3. Select "License" from the menu.
4. Enter your license key in the screen provided.
5. You're all set! You'll get free updates for life.  

## Thanks again!

We hope you love Mic Drop! If you have any questions, concerns, or bug reports, please do feel free to check our [support pages](/help) or [contact us](mailto:help@getmicdrop.com), and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
