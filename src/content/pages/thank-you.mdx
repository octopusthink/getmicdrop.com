Thank you so much for buying Mic Drop! ❤️ Your purchase supports independent software developers and helps fund new features. 

## Getting your license

You should receive a receipt and a license key via email momentarily. If it doesn't come through (check your spam folders just in case!), please [drop us a line](mailto:help@getmicdrop.com) and we'll get you sorted. 

## How to activate your license

1. <DownloadLink source="post-purchase">Download Mic Drop</DownloadLink>, if you haven't already.
2. Open Mic Drop and click on the mic icon in the menu bar to open the menu.
3. Select "License" from the menu.
4. Enter your email and license key in the license screen.
5. You're all set! You'll get free updates for life.  

## Questions, feedback, and bug reports

We hope you love Mic Drop as much as we've enjoyed building it.

If you have any questions, concerns, or bug reports, please do feel free to check our [support pages](/help) or [contact us](mailto:help@getmicdrop.com), and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
